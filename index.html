Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last against increasingly challenging waves of monsters.

Key Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • Character progression system
  • Multiple enemy types
  • Dynamic difficulty scaling

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become - but beware, the monsters become stronger too!
